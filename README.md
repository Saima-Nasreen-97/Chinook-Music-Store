# Chinook-Music-Store-Analysis

## Problem
Chinook wanted to analyze music store sales data to understand customer behavior, sales trends, and churn patterns. As an analyst, I analyzed the database to provide actionable recommendations for improving customer retention and revenue growth.

## Key Findings
- Rock dominates USA market – 53% of total sales
- 27% customer churn rate (16 of 59 customers inactive in last 6 months)
- Customers with $100+ spend or 13+ purchases have 0% churn
- $60-$99 LTV segment is highest churn risk zone
- Rock + Metal purchased together 1,622 times
- Top 5 countries account for 59% of customers
- Single-customer countries show 100% churn rate

## Recommendations
- Set VIP loyalty threshold at $100 spend or 13 purchases
- Target $60-$99 LTV customers for retention campaigns
- Bundle Rock with Metal tracks for cross-selling
- Launch win-back campaigns for 16 churned customers
- Add age, gender, and release year columns to database
